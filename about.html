Our Mission

We're a community of food-lovers on a mission to reinvent the traditional restaurant experience. We love dining out at top restaurants, but we crave a different sort of restaurant experience. We want dinner out to be a social experience that tells a story. We love trying lots of different dishes, and don't want to have to choose what to order. Rather than having chefs hide in kitchens, we want chefs to come out, talk about their journeys with food, and share the stories behind the dishes they serve.

Our Dining Events

We create two different types of dining events: Feasts and Tastings. Our feasts are seated communal dinners. Diners come together around shared tables to enjoy multi-course menus that showcase the chefs' favorite dishes, and chefs' come out to introduce the dishes. Our tastings follow the same format in that chefs craft the menus and come out to introduce the dishes, but the event format is standing cocktail-type receptions rather than seated dinners.

Our Member Benefits

We negotiate exclusive privileges for our members from a curated selection of the best food and lifestyle brands. From artisanal purveyors to top restaurants to food-tech services, members enjoy preferred treatment and insider savings unavailable to the general public.
