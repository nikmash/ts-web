Chefs come out to share the stories behind the meals. Learn where ingredients are sourced from and how dishes are made.

Chefs craft multi-course meals that showcase their favorite dishes - typically a mix of restaurant staples and off-menu creations.

What to Expect

We work with top restaurants to create social dining experiences that challenge conventions of how dinner out is supposed to be. Chefs serve up 6+ dishes and come out to talk food. Ticket prices typically range from $40 to $55, which covers all of the food.
